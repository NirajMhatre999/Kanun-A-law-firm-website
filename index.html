Our Practices Areas

Civil Law

Civil Law deals with cases where wrong is done against a particular individual.

Family Law

Family law is an area of the law that deals with family matters and domestic relations.

Business Law

Commercial law or business law is the body of law which governs business and commerce.

Education Law

The Education law is a branch of Civil Law, dealing with laws, regulations, policies, and instructions.

Criminal Law

Criminal law is the body of law that relates to crime. It prescribes conduct perceived as threatening.

Cyber Law

Cyber law is a term used to describe the legal issues related to use of communications technology.
